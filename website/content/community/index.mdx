# Documentation

Welcome to the OpenBao community documentation! This documentation is your
guide to the OpenBao community and supplements documents available on GitHub.

It contains information about contributing to OpenBao, deprecation notices,
community policies, release notes on past versions, and RFCs put forward by
the community as a whole.

In addition, the following documents might be of interest:

 - The overall [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](https://github.com/openbao/openbao/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md)
   which applies to all repositories.
 - Our [`MAINTAINERS.md`](https://github.com/openbao/openbao/blob/main/MAINTAINERS.md).
 - The OpenBao [TSC governance document](https://github.com/openbao/openbao/blob/main/GOVERNANCE.md).
 - The OpenBao [Dev WG Charter](https://github.com/openbao/dev-wg/blob/main/CHARTER.md).
